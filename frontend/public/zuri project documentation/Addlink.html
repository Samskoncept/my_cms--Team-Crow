Adding social Links


Add social links to display social icons to your visitors,
helping them find you wherever you maintain a presence
on the web. Icons include logos for many common
platforms, like Facebook, instagram, and Twitter. You can
also add link or mail icons that lead to any website or
email address.



Add social Links


Add links to the social links panel to display social icons
across your site.

. If we recognize the platform based on the URL, we'll
display an icon with the platform's logo.

. If the link is from a page or service we dont recognize,
it'll be represented by a link icon.

. If the link is an email address, it'll be represented by a
mail icon.
