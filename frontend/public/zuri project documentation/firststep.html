First steps

Every CROW site begins on a free subscription. If you are just visiting us at CROW, there is a possibility for you to set up a website. You click the Browse Template button on the homepage and click Create Website, fill in some information and immediately you have a website setup for you with that information. You also have access to preview the templates on the website. To have access to editing these beautifully curated templates you would have to join CROW by registering to edit templates and also create a custom website for free. As a registered user you can explore the platform, upload content, experiment with your design, and share your progress with friends and colleagues. When you get started, you start with a template of your choice. Templates help to showcase site features and give you an idea of how your site can be styled. You can make changes to the template by adding images, customizing colors, adding blocks, changing fonts and so much more.
